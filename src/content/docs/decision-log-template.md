# Decision Log Template

## Decision
What was decided.

## Context
What situation led to the decision.

## Why
Why this choice was made.

## Alternatives Considered
What else was considered and why it was rejected.

## Consequences
What this changes.

## Revisit Trigger
What would make us revisit this later.

## Good prompts
- Turn this into a decision log entry.
- Add this decision to the docs.
- Summarize the tradeoff and log the decision.
